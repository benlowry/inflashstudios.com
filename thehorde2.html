Play The Horde 2

For a long time now I've been working on trying to bring the fun of tower defense games in Warcraft 3 to Flash. The Horde 2 is my second attempt at doing that, it has a load of stuff you don't usually see in Flash games like a minimap and large levels that you can scroll around on. Max Games was gracious enough to sponsor this for me. If you haven't played tower defense in Warcraft 3 you should go buy a copy because it is so much more fun than Flash games.... even this one.
